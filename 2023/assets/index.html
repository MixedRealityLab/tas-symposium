First International Symposium on Trustworthy Autonomous Systems

Welcome!

The Trustworthy Autonomous Systems 2023 symposium focuses on novel and creative multidisciplinary responsible research & innovation of trustworthy autonomous systems)..

Welcome to TAS ’23

TAS '23 features nine sessions taking place over two days, including presentation of 26 full papers, 31 posters, and TAS Creative Provocations.

You’ll have opportunity to network and hear talks from current TAS researchers as they showcase creative, multi- and interdisciplinary responsible research & innovation which focuses on the challenging question of how to ensure that the design, engineering, and operation of autonomous systems generates positive outcomes and mitigates potentially harmful outcomes for people, societies, economies and the environment.

TAS ’23 takes place at Heriot-Watt University in Edinburgh, the home of the TAS Node in Trust and the newly built National Robotarium.
