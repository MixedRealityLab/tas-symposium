Poster printing services for TAS '23 presenters

HWU Media Services have offered printing services on campus.

To access these services, symposium attendees should register for an account on the HWU Media Services store.

Media services recommend 7 days grace for printing, and posters should be collected from: Gait 6, Heriot-Watt University, Edinburgh Campus (Building G on the campus map).
